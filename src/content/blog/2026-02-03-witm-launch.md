# WITM is Live!

We are incredibly excited to announce that **WITM - Who Is The Most** is now officially available on both the **App Store** and **Google Play**!

## What is WITM?

WITM is a fun and interactive party game that brings friends together. Create a room, invite your friends, and answer entertaining "Who is the most...?" questions. Vote for each other and discover surprising truths about your friend group!

## Key Features

- **Create & Join Rooms** - Set up a private game room in seconds and invite friends with a simple PIN code.
- **Fun Questions** - Explore dozens of categories from Personality to Leadership, with new questions added regularly.
- **Real-Time Voting** - Cast your votes and see results unfold live.
- **Leaderboard** - Track scores and compete with your friends.

## Made in Switzerland

WITM was imagined, developed, and refined in Fribourg, Switzerland. We are a small team with big ideas, and we are committed to listening to our community.
